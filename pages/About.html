The Nimbus 4 spans 26.4 m and had its first flight in 1990, since which it has been equipped with various engines, enhancing its independence. With a glide ratio of about 60, the Nimbus showcases outstanding flight performance and excellent slow flight characteristics.

Model Design

Our model is designed at a 1:5 scale to replicate the original's flight dynamics as closely as possible. It provides the pilot with a true thermal flying experience, responding well to the slightest updrafts and translating them into altitude. The Nimbus can be flown both comfortably and dynamically.

The model's required basic speed is very low, enabling precise and stress-free landings, even on relatively small areas, thanks to its spoiler and flaps.
